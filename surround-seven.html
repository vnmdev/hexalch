How it works
  • The ladder is the classic transmutation order: Lead → Tin → Iron → Copper → Quicksilver → Silver → Gold (Saturn, Jupiter, Mars, Venus, Mercury, Moon, Sun). Solve a tier to ascend; boards grow and harden as you climb.
  • The board is a hexagon of cells. Each clue counts greens in its 7-cell cluster (itself + 6 neighbours; fewer on the rim). Clue numbers are inked in the current metal's colour.
  • Generation: a random solution is made, every cell's clue computed, then clues are stripped as long as a deduction-only solver can still rebuild the whole board. The result is a unique, no-guess puzzle.
  • Progress is saved in your browser, so you resume at your current metal. Completed tiers stay unlocked — click one to replay it.
